What PolicyBot is

PolicyBot takes 8 inputs about your product (company name, what you collect, third-party services, target geography, age of users, etc.) and assembles a Privacy Policy and Terms of Service from common, widely-used clause patterns. The output is delivered as HTML and PDF.

The clause patterns are inspired by frameworks like the GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA — meaning they cover the topics those regulations typically require — but inclusion of a topic is not the same as compliance. Compliance depends on your actual data practices, your contracts with third parties, your cookie banners, your data-deletion processes, and a lot of other things outside the scope of a document.

When to consult an attorney instead

You should engage a qualified attorney — not just a template — if any of the following apply to you:

  • You handle health information (HIPAA), financial data (GLBA, PCI-DSS), or children's data under 13 (COPPA).
  • You operate in the EU/UK and process personal data of EU/UK residents at scale.
  • You are a regulated industry: healthcare, finance, insurance, legal, education, telecom, gambling, alcohol, firearms, cannabis.
  • You are raising venture capital or pursuing an acquisition where due diligence requires reviewed policies.
  • You sell to government or enterprise customers with required vendor compliance documentation.
  • You have users in California exercising CCPA rights (deletion, portability, sale opt-out) and need procedures, not just disclosures.
  • You receive a regulator inquiry, a data subject request, or a breach.
